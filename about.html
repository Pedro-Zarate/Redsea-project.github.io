About

This website was created as part of a personal project while learning software development, it was designed and created by myself. I thought this would be a cool way to remember the types of species of animals and corals that we keep, so I built this webpage. Now friends and family can also learn about our small piece of the ocean, by simply scanning a QR code on the tank that directs them to this website.

FAQ: You can learn more about the fish by double-clicking on the image of the one you're interested in on the fish page. This website is designed to open up a new tab on your phone to an article about that fish.
